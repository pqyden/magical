e-Commerce

E-commerce, is the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet. It allows businesses and consumers to conduct transactions online using various payment methods, such as credit cards, debit cards, and electronic checks. E-commerce has grown significantly in recent years and has become an important part of many businesses' operations. It offers a convenient way for customers to shop and for businesses to reach a wider market.

SEO DIGITAL MARKETING

SEO, is optimizing a website to improve its ranking in search engine results pages. This is done through a variety of techniques. The goal of SEO marketing is to improve the visibility and ranking of a website in search engines, for specific keywords related to the business or products/services offered. When a website ranks higher in search results, it is more likely to receive traffic from potential customers who are searching for the products/services the website offers.
